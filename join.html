What to expect

Your first seven days.

Joining is one click. Here's what happens after that.

  1. Day 1 — Welcome

    You land in the community, set up your profile, and meet a welcome team member.

  2. Day 2–3 — Orient

    You're guided through the spaces that matter most for your sector and destination.

  3. Day 4–5 — Connect

    Introduced to peers already on a similar path — and to a recruiter if your readiness profile matches.

  4. Day 6–7 — Start a journey

    If you're ready, the structured pathway begins. If not, you continue learning at your own pace.
