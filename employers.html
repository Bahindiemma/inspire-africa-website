The status quo

International hiring is broken.

You pay more — but outcomes don't improve. Legacy recruitment passes risk down the chain to you.

  • ×

    Long hiring timelines

    Months lost between vacancy and arrival, while your roster runs short.

  • ×

    High and unpredictable costs

    Fees scale with friction, not with quality. You don't know the final number until the invoice arrives.

  • ×

    Poor candidate matching

    CVs aren't readiness. Mismatched hires drop out in the first 90 days.

  • ×

    Opaque subcontractor networks

    You don't know who actually sourced the worker, or what they paid to get there.

A better way to hire

Built for outcomes — not transactions.

INSPIRE AFRICA replaces fragmented recruitment with a structured, Africa-based talent pipeline. The model is transparent, ethical and measurable end to end.

    Centralised preparation

    Candidates arrive prepared for the workplace — language, documentation, expectations all handled.

  • ✓

Five steps. No surprises.

From defining your hiring needs to ongoing post-placement support — a coordinated, predictable process.

  1. Define your hiring needs

    Roles, sectors, locations, scale, timeline.

  2. Receive pre-screened candidates

    Predictive screening shortlists those who will actually arrive ready.

  3. Interview and select

    Your decision, on your timeline, with full context on each candidate.
